CCIS Innovision 2026 Final Project

PumpScout Davao

View Prototype →

PumpScout Davao is an Android app for fuel station discovery, crowdsourced price reporting, and ESP32-based crowd monitoring at gas stations. It combines real-time community-reported fuel prices with a Linear Regression forecasting model (Flask API on Render, MAE ₱0.48, MAPE under 1%) to help drivers in Davao find the cheapest and least crowded stations nearby. Built with Flutter, Kotlin, Firebase, Mapbox, OpenStreetMap/Overpass API, Geolocator, Room Database, and Cloudinary.

Reflection

Winning Best Smart Monitoring Solution at CCIS Innovision 2026 was a validating moment for our team after months of building PumpScout Davao. Presenting the ESP32 crowd-monitoring hardware alongside the forecasting model in front of judges pushed us to explain not just what the app does, but why each design choice mattered, from sensor placement to the accuracy of our price predictions. It reinforced how much clearer communication becomes when you deeply understand your own system. Working with Abusaman, Arigo, and Frani through the deadlines, debugging sessions, and last-minute fixes also taught me that a strong final product depends as much on team coordination as it does on individual skill. This recognition motivates us to keep refining PumpScout Davao beyond the classroom.
